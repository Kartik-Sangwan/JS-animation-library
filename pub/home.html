At-Headings.js

The best animation library that you need for dynamic and vibrant Headings. Gone are the days when you use plain h1,h2 tags and just color it to have a boring headings.

Animaitons

There are a variety of animations including animated headings, lots of customization options such as speed, color, size, shape, animation effects such as slant heading, scatter and Comeback, transform headings,and obviously the basic display on the native canvas.

Backgrounds

The library also provides a variety of cool and animated background which have different particles and many different behaviours that can be mix-matched together to create a fully customizable canvas background specific for your website.

Mouse Trail Effects

Another special functionality is the use of special mouse pointer animations which can be used during presentations and other meetings. Most of the functionalities have been achieved via DOM manipulation and the developer has full freedom to customize any part of the API.

PARTICLES SECTION

Enter the heading that you want to be displayed!

How to use this section properly:

  1. Please don't enter any other character other than A-Z or a-z as the library currently only supports alphabets.

  2. Only slant animation can be stopped as it is a infinite animation. Click on stop animation to stop it and then clear canvas to try some other animation. Other animations are timed which can be specified by the developer and thus can't be stopped in the middle .

  3. After clicking on the fleeSingle animation hover your mouse over the heading to see the visual effect.
  4. After clicking on the fleeAll animation hover the mouse over the first letter of the heading to see all the characters flee away from the mouse in harmony.
  5. The change color and change size option once clicked doesn't show anything but the size and colors are updated and on the subsequent animation these settings will be applied. Try out a animation after selecting these options.
